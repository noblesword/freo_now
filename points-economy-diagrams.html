üí° How It Works

Business purchases points at a 10% markup: They pay $0.011 per point (1.1 cents) which has a face value of $0.01 (1 cent) to customers. This gives the platform $0.001 per point in revenue.

Points issued from float: When a customer makes a purchase, the business issues points from their pre-purchased float. Customer earns 1 point per dollar spent.
